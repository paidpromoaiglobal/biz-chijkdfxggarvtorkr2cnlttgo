About Us

MARI YOGA CENTRE

Mari Yoga Centre, located near the Government Stadium on Bypass Road in Karaikal, Puducherry, is a premier destination for holistic wellness and yoga practice. With a perfect 5-star rating from over 83 satisfied students, our centre has built a strong reputation for providing a calm, welcoming, and deeply enriching yoga experience for all age groups — from beginners to advanced practitioners.

Our experienced and friendly yoga masters are dedicated to guiding every student with patience, encouragement, and personalised attention. Whether you are looking to improve your physical fitness, mental clarity, or overall well-being, Mari Yoga Centre offers a nurturing environment that makes yoga accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We also have a branch in Thirunallar, making quality yoga education even more accessible to the community.

  • Perfect 5-Star Rating from 83+ Happy Students
  • Expert, Patient & Friendly Yoga Masters
  • Open 6 Days a Week from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM
  • Suitable for All Age Groups Including Kids & Beginners
  • Two Convenient Locations — Karaikal & Thirunallar
  • Peaceful & Relaxing Environment for Effective Practice
